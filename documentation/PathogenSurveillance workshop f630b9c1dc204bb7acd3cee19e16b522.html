What is Gitpod?

We are using Gitpod to provide hands-on experience of installing and using the pathogen surveillance pipeline we developed. Gitpod¬†allows you to launch and enter Virtual Machines from your browser and gives you 50 free hours per calendar month, enough for this session.

How do I sign up for Gitpod?

You MUST do the following steps before the workshop starts! It will take a few minutes each to set up a¬†GitHub¬†account and to set up a Gitpod account if you don't already have them:
